## Design Context

### Users
- Broad audience of couples (new, long-term, and married) using mobile devices.
- Primary context: busy routines where date planning gets deprioritized.
- Core job to be done: consistently plan and complete meaningful dates together.

### Brand Personality
- Premium, romantic, and supportive.
- Encouraging accountability without pressure or shame.
- Emotional goal: help couples feel intentional and connected.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Mobile-first experience for iOS and Android.
- Elegant and warm visual style, not playful-gamified or hyper-competitive.
- Social exposure is excluded from MVP; experience is private and couple-centric.

### Design Principles
1. Consistency over intensity: optimize for weekly habits and long-term streak stability.
2. Romantic clarity: keep interactions simple, emotionally warm, and visually premium.
3. Gentle accountability: reminders and streaks should motivate, not pressure.
4. Fast planning flow: reduce friction from "idea" to "scheduled date" in a few steps.
5. Privacy by default: couple data stays private in MVP.
